Concept

Luppio is an audio-visual experience made in the Creative Coding course at Politecnico di Milano. In this project the experience of taking care of plants is taken to the next level: users can be create their own alien plant. Enchantment is delivered through a visual and interactive experience that makes people forget their daily struggles for a few moments. We made use of technologies such as the hand gesture detection, the Json Archive, the brightness and color tone detection and the voice level analysis.
