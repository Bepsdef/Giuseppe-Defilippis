Concept

We were asked to devolop a set of graphic means that aim to prevent or instruct about Covid19. We’ve analyzed Covid-19 in its complexity in order to find a useful, peculiar and indistinct message to communicate. Eating a healthy diet is very important during the COVID-19 pandemic. What we eat and drink can affect our body’s ability to prevent, fight and recover from infections. [WHO] Encouraging people to have a healthy and balanced diet is the aim of our campaign. Eat your veggies
